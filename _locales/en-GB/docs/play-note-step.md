# 🎵 Play Note Step

## What does it do?
This block works like an automatic piano key! It plays a musical note from the C Major scale when you give it a number. 

## How do you use it?
You give the block a number from **0 to 18**. 
- `0` is the starting note, **G**.
- Every time you count up (1, 2, 3...), the note goes higher in pitch! 

You can also click the little `+` symbol to turn on a **wobbly sound** (vibrato) which makes your note shake back and forth like an electric guitar string!

## Why do we use it?
Instead of needing to memorize complex frequencies (like 440 Hertz) or how to read sheet music, we use simple numbers. This lets you write cool songs just by doing basic math or using loops to count from 0 to 18!
