# 🎵 Play Continuous Note

## What does it do?
This block acts like an alarm or a ringtone. It turns on a musical note and keeps it playing forever in the background while the rest of your code keeps running!

## How do you use it?
Give the block a number from **0 to 18** to pick your pitch (0 is G, and higher numbers mean higher pitch). 

Because this block runs in the **background**, it will not freeze your code. If you put a "show icon" block right underneath it, the icon will appear immediately while the music sings!

⚠️ **Warning:** The music will never stop on its own! You must use the `🛑 stop continuous music` block when you want it to be quiet.

## Why do we use it?
Sometimes you want background music playing while a game is running, or you want a continuous siren to sound while your robot is driving. This block lets the micro:bit do two things at once!
