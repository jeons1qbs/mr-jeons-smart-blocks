# 🛑 Stop Continuous Music

## What does it do?
This block instantly mutes any continuous ringtones or wobbly vibrato sounds that you started playing from the QBS Music menu.

## How do you use it?
Snap this block anywhere you want the sound to end. For example, you might start a continuous note when `Button A` is pressed, and use this Stop block when `Button B` is pressed.

## Why do we use it?
Without a "Stop" block, continuous background music would screech forever until you unplugged the micro:bit's battery! We use this to regain silence.
